##  Reflection

In this task, I created my digital profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and kaggle . Before this, I did not realize how important it is to have a proper online presence as a student. Setting up these profiles helped me understand how professionals present their skills and achievements online.

GitHub helped me learn how to organize my work and share projects, while LinkedIn showed me how to build a professional profile that can be seen by recruiters and companies. It was a new experience for me to fill in details like education, skills, and interests in a proper way.

One thing I found slightly challenging was understanding what information should be included and how to keep it professional. Overall, this task helped me take the first step toward building my digital identity, and I plan to keep updating these profiles regularly in the future.
