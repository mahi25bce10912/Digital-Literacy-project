##  Project Overview
This project is designed as a **Digital Literacy Portfolio**, where I take on the role of a *Student Digital Ambassador*. The aim is to explore and demonstrate essential digital skills required in today’s academic and professional environment.

Through this project, I have worked on multiple tasks such as creating awareness content, building a professional online presence, understanding collaboration tools, practising email etiquette, and learning about cyber safety. Each task reflects practical knowledge that can be applied in real-life situations.

---

##  Module Summaries

###  Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness
Created an infographic to explain the concept of digital literacy, including safe internet practices, useful tools, and maintaining a professional online presence.

---

###  Module 2: Digital Portfolio Development
Built and updated professional profiles on platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn. This module helped in understanding how to present oneself effectively in the digital world.

---

###  Module 3: Platforms & Collaboration Tools
Explored coding platforms and completed beginner-level challenges. Also created a Google Form quiz to understand collaborative tools and data collection.

---

###  Module 4: Email Etiquette & Communication
Drafted professional emails and created a checklist for responsible social media usage. This module focused on improving formal communication skills.

---

###  Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness
Studied a cybercrime case and created a prevention checklist. This helped in understanding online threats and how to stay safe in the digital environment.
