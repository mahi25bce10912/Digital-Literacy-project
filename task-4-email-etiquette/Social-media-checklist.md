##Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
---
##Do’s
---
- Always think carefully before posting anything online.
- Communicate politely and respectfully with others.
- Maintain a clean and professional profile image.
- Keep your personal data private and secure.
- Share only verified and trustworthy information.
 ---
##Don’ts
---
- Avoid spreading unverified news or misinformation.
- Do not use rude, abusive, or harmful language.
- Never post confidential or sensitive details.
- Stay away from unnecessary online conflicts.
- Do not reveal too much about your personal life.
