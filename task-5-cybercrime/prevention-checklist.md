# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist 

- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or trusted service.

- Always check payment requests carefully on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe. Remember, entering your PIN means you are sending money.

- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links received through SMS, email, or WhatsApp.

- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts like email, social media, and banking.

- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts for extra security.

- Avoid logging into sensitive accounts while using public Wi-Fi in cafes, libraries, or college campuses.

- Do not share too much personal information (phone number, address, college details) on social media.

- Regularly check your bank statements and UPI transactions to notice any unusual activity.

- In case of cyber fraud, report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call helpline 1930.

### Financial & UPI Safety Tips
- Remember that you do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money  
- Always double-check payment details before sending money to anyone  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes, as they may lead to fraudulent transactions  

### Reporting Cybercrime in India
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call Cybercrime Helpline: **1930**
